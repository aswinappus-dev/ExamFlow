About ExamFlow

ExamFlow is a modern, automated solution designed to simplify the complex and often tedious task of exam hall seating arrangement. Born from the need to create a fair, efficient, and transparent system, ExamFlow eliminates manual errors and biases by using a powerful randomization engine.

Our platform provides dedicated portals for students to quickly find their seats, for invigilators to access hall layouts, and for administrators to manage all aspects of the examination process. By automating scheduling, randomization, and data cleanup, ExamFlow ensures a seamless and stress-free experience for educational institutions.
