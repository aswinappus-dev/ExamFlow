Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I can't find my seat. What should I do?

A: Seating arrangements are typically revealed a few hours before the exam. Please check back closer to the exam time. If the issue persists after the reveal time, please contact your examination department directly.

Q: The website shows "No upcoming exam scheduled." Why?

A: This message appears when the administrator has not yet scheduled or confirmed an exam for randomization. The data will become available once an exam is confirmed in the system.

Contact Information

For technical difficulties or urgent support, please contact the IT department at your institution. For general inquiries about the ExamFlow platform, you can reach out to our team at: support@examflow.com.
